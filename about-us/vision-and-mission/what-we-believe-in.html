We are dedicated to delivering a hi-tech trading environment to our clients, wherever they are

Our mission is to participate in the development of a culture of investing in financial markets in the European Union, as one of the leading retail brokerage houses. We work towards achieving this goal by developing our investment services on the basis of cutting-edge trading technology, seeking the possibilities for innovation in everything we do and building fair relationships with our closest environment.

Technology
We believe that technology is the key factor when challenging the dynamics of the modern financial markets. What we provide to our clients is the trading platform of the next generation, that is capable of handling even the most sophisticated investment strategies.

Clear Rules
We believe that clearly defined rules and mutual respect constitute the basis to build fair and honest relations with our closest environment – our clients, partners and employees.

Team Spirit
For us the care for the development and satisfaction of our employees is the key to success. Our employees are passionate and knowledgeable, providing our clients with the support whenever it is needed.
